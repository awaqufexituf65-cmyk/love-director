# Love Director — X/Twitter Thread

## EN Version

1/ 🎬 I built an open-source "Love Director" for Claude Code.

It doesn't give dating advice. It doesn't detect scammers. It shows you YOUR relationship patterns — and stages multiple possible futures based on your actual chat data.

2/ Here's the problem: most dating content is either "how to attract" (manipulation) or "how to spot red flags" (fear).

Neither helps you understand YOURSELF — the patterns YOU keep repeating across relationships.

3/ Love Director does 5 things:

1. Builds a 3-layer profile of YOU (what you SAY vs what your chat data SHOWS)
2. Probes your dating philosophy across 7 dimensions
3. Extracts core patterns from your behavior
4. Generates a probabilistic DECISION TREE of possible futures
5. Lets YOU decide which path you want

4/ The decision tree is the key. It doesn't give generic "you should communicate more" advice.

It says: "Based on YOUR data, if you keep avoiding conflict (72% probability), combined with YOUR pattern of choosing high-conflict partners (35% conditional), here's what happens..."

5/ Each ending has:
- 📊 Probability + data source
- ⏱️ Timeframe
- 📖 A specific story using YOUR details
- ⚖️ What you gain vs. what you lose

6 types of endings: Inertia, Growth, Surprise, Warning, Cycle, Reconstruction.

5/ The knowledge base compiles data from 14+ sources:
- Chinese Academy of Sciences (55,781 sample)
- Fudan University social mindset report
- BNU × Xiaohongshu joint study
- Douyin dating white paper
- Soul × Fudan social trends report

6/ Privacy-first by design:
- No data storage
- No network calls
- Chat records analyzed in-session only
- `allowed-tools: Read, AskUserQuestion, TodoRead, TodoWrite`

7/ It also integrates with external tools for deeper analysis:
- WeFlow (WeChat export)
- wechat_analysis (sentiment curves)
- Lila MCP (attachment style assessment)
- ex-skill (partner personality distillation)

8/ Open source, MIT license.
Cross-platform: Claude Code / Codex / OpenClaw / Cursor / Gemini CLI
